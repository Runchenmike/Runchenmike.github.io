My Current Research Direction

Right now my direction of interest is the two-dimensional materials part of condensed matter physics. I am very interested in multilayer material stacks and their interactions and effects. Let's imagine a world of nanometers and micrometers where materials and atoms that are too small to be real interact with each other to form promising and interesting physical phenomena. And I had a project is about graphene, the king of the 2D material, I'm currently interested in the moire spots it forms with hexagonal boron nitride in the same lattice orientation, because it would have interesting phenomena like superlattice and superconductivity.
